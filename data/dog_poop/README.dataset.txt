Object detection identifying two categorization types of dog poo: Healthy and HGE (hemorrhagic gastroenteritis) stool. HGE stool is a sign of potentially fatal condition and dog should be given immediate medical care.

--

Healthy stool: brown color, compact log shape
HGE stool: reddish streaks, jelly consistency
